MATERIALS:

All materials are with a 30º smoothing, except:

ZA-black black:          0º
ZA-blue:		60º
ZA-grey matte:		40º
ZA-leather:		89º
ZA-light glass:		89º
ZA-light glassIN:	89º
ZA-orange:		60º
 
ZA-light glass: refraction index: 1.35
ZA-light glassIN: refraction index: 1.0

Apply the image map HITACHIorange.jpg to the material ZA-orange.
Apply the image map HITACHIblue.jpg to the material ZA-blue.

The object is modeled "closed", you do not need to apply double sided material in any polygon.
